Smart Parking System using IoT

A real-time IoT-based parking management system featuring live parking availability tracking, vehicle search, automated gate access via license plate recognition, and secure digital payments. Designed to enhance parking efficiency at Kluang Mall, ensuring a seamless and convenient experience while reducing congestion.
